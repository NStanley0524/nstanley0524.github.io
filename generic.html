ABOUT ME

With over three years of experience in data analytics, Nwajagu Stanley Ifeanyi is a dynamic professional known for transforming raw data into actionable insights that drive business success. His proficiency in Python, SQL, Power BI, Tableau and Excel enables him to analyze complex datasets, develop robust solutions, and deliver data-driven strategies.

Stanley’s diverse industry experience spans Retail and EdTech , where he has not only contributed to business growth but also demonstrated a remarkable ability to communicate complex data concepts clearly. As a seasoned trainer, Stanley has trained over 500 professionals through engaging and high-quality training sessions in data analysis. His passion lies in equipping individuals with essential data skills, enhancing learning experiences, and fostering growth through the power of analytics.

I combine analytical expertise with strong communication and collaboration skills. I excel at translating complex datasets into simple, actionable narratives that business leaders can easily understand and act upon. My adaptability, attention to detail, and problem-solving mindset have enabled me to thrive in dynamic environments and deliver consistent value. As a mentor and trainer, I also enjoy empowering junior analysts and non-technical stakeholders to become more data literate, creating a culture where data drives decisions.

My technical toolkit includes Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, BeautifulSoup, Matplotlib, Seaborn) for analytics and automation, SQL & PostgreSQL for data querying and modeling, and Excel (advanced functions, Power Query, VBA) for business reporting. I design insightful dashboards and interactive reports in Power BI, and I’m skilled in building data pipelines, conducting predictive modeling, and performing statistical analyses. My ability to integrate these tools ensures seamless end-to-end solutions—from raw data extraction and cleaning to advanced analytics and visualization.

Over the years, I’ve applied my skills across multiple industries including healthcare, education technology, and retail. I have developed predictive models to estimate housing prices, conducted survival and mortality analysis for healthcare datasets, automated financial reporting workflows, and designed dimensional models for sales data warehousing. In my current role as a Senior Data Analyst at 10Alytics, I lead training programs for international students, guiding them through real-world analytics projects and ensuring they acquire practical, industry-relevant skills. My work demonstrates a balance between technical depth and strategic impact, always focused on enabling data-driven decision-making.
